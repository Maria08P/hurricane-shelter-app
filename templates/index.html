🤝 Community: Help Your Neighbor

Recovery is faster when we look out for each other. Use this checklist to support your street:

  • Elderly/Disabled: Check on neighbors who live alone or have mobility issues.
  • Debris: Help clear storm drains to prevent localized street flooding.
  • Resources: If you have extra water or a working chainsaw, offer a hand.
